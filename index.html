About Rohit Modern Junior High School 🏫

Founded in 2005, Rohit Modern Junior High School is a co-educational, English-medium school affiliated with the CBSE curriculum, offering classes from Grade 1 to Grade 10. Located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, the school is dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping confident, disciplined, and responsible individuals.

At Rohit Modern Junior High School, education goes beyond academics. We focus on the all-round development of every child—mentally, physically, and socially. Our students enjoy a wide range of activities designed to enhance skills, fitness, and creativity. These include swimming 🏊, skating ⛸️, cycling 🚴, judo karate, dance, mehendi art, Olympiad competitions, summer camps, English speaking classes, and civil defence training. Our playgrounds, activity halls, and specialized labs provide an ideal environment for children to explore their interests and talents.

The school also offers more than 80 well-maintained classrooms, a well-stocked library with over 1,000 books, and a modern computer-aided learning lab 💻 to foster curiosity, creativity, and digital skills. Separate sanitation facilities and a secure pucca boundary wall ensure a safe and supportive environment for all students.

Our mission is to inspire each child to reach their full potential, develop their abilities, and prepare them for a bright and successful future, academically, physically, and socially. 🌟
